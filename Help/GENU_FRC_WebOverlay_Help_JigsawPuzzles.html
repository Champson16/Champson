Jigsaw Puzzles Options Help

Create jigsaw puzzles from the "U of Chew" original artwork. As an added bonus, whenever you save a picture in the U of Chew Art Department, it automatically becomes available as a puzzle. Create as many puzzles as you want!

After you've chosen your settings, tap Start Puzzle to begin solving the puzzle.

Jigsaw Puzzle Setup

This screen allows you to choose the image, the number of pieces in the puzzle, whether the pieces should rotate, and whether special effects will be applied. All of these choices allow you to create a puzzle that is just the right difficulty for you.

You can choose any of the background images from the storybook or any image you have created in the Art Department. Tap any image to select it.

The image chosen for the puzzle is highlighted with a bright white border.

Special FX

None - Turns off all special effects.

Night Vision - Turns the image from color to black and white.

Fizzle Fuzzle - Continually distorts the image.

Emboss - Turns the colors gray and raises the design.

Sketch - Changes the image into a sketch.

Wobble - Puzzle pieces will wobble as you try to piece them back together.

Vintage - Image instantly becomes an old photograph.

 

More Options

Beneath the puzzle image are two interactive controls:

Pieces Slider - Move the slider to control the number of pieces in the puzzle.

Tap to Rotate - For an added challenge, turn this option ON to have puzzle pieces randomly rotated when you start. Tap the pieces to rotate back to the correct perspective.

 

Controls

In addition, there are options in the Action and Settings Bars.

To access either the Action or Settings bar, tap the U of Chew icon in the upper left and a Gear icon in the upper right of the screen:

Action Bar

The Action Bar offers options for navigating through the U of Chew app.

Tap the U of Chew icon and the following appears:

 

If you don't wish to choose any of the options, tap anywhere on the screen and the Action Bar disappears.

Home - Tap the Home icon to return to the Title Screen.

 

Options - When you are solving the puzzle, tap the Options icon to change the settings on your puzzle.

 

FatRedCouch - Tap the FatRedCouch icon to view other apps available for your device.

 

Start Over - When you are solving a puzzle, the Start Over icon is used to reset and start over.

 

Help - Tap the Help icon to view this page again.

 

Settings Bar

The Settings Bar offers options for controlling some features.

Tap the Gears icon and options will appear.

If you don't wish to choose any of the options, tap anywhere on screen and the Action Bar will disappear.

 

Timer - When you are solving a puzzle, tap the Timer icon to turn on/off the timer displayed while you are solving a puzzle. Try to beat your time!

Puzzle Preview - When you are solving a puzzle, tap the Puzzle Preview icon to turn on/off the background puzzle image, increasing the level of difficulty.

Music - Tap the Music icon to turn on/off the background music.

 

Jigsaw Puzzles Help

Once you have set your options and tapped Start Puzzle, the solve puzzle screen appears, disperses the pieces, and starts the timer.

Move the pieces in place to solve the puzzle.

When the puzzle is solved, the timer stops and a congratulatory message is heard.

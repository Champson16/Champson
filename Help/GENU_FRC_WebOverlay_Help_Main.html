Controls

Tap on screen and the U of Chew icon in the upper left for the Action Bar and a Gear icon in the upper right for the Settings Bar

Tap the U of Chew icon in the upper left for the Action Bar and a Gear icon in the upper right for the Settings Bar:

Action Bar

Tap the U of Chew icon and the Action Bar appears offering options for navigating through the U of Chew app. These vary depending on where you are in the app.

If you don't wish to choose any of the options, tap anywhere on screen and the Action Bar disappears.

Help - View the Help.

 

Settings Bar

Tap the Gears icon and the Settings Bar appears offering options for controlling the features of the app. Different options appear depending on what section of the app your are in.

Music - Turn on/off the background music.

Concentration Game Setup Help

Concentration is a card game in which all the cards are laid face down and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards in the least amount of turns. Once you know where certain cards are located, use your memory to remember where you have already seen its match.

Concentration Game Setup

This screen allows you to choose whether the game will use 12 or 24 cards. To begin the game, choose the number of cards to use. In addition, there are options in both the Action and Settings Bars.

Controls

To access either the Action or Settings bar, tap the U of Chew icon in the upper left or the Gear icon in the upper right:

Action Bar

The Action Bar offers options for navigation.

Tap the U of Chew icon and the following appears:

If you don't wish to choose any of the options, tap anywhere on the screen and the Action Bar disappears.

Home - Return to the Title Screen.

Options - Returns you to the start screen, so you can change the number of cards to play.

 

FatRedCouch - View other apps available for your device.

 

Start Over - Reset the game when you are playing.

Help - View the Help.

 

Settings Bar

The Settings Bar offers options for controlling some features.

Tap the Gears icon and the following appears:

If you don't wish to choose any of the options, tap anywhere on screen and the Settings Bar will disappear.

Match Counter - Turn on/off a counter displayed while playing. See how fast you can win the game!

 

Music - Turn on/off the background music.
